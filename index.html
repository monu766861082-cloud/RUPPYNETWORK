What is R U P Network?

R U P Network is a free rewards and engagement platform launched in 2026. Our mission is to help users earn reward points through simple daily activities. No investment, purchase, or payment is ever required. Users collect points by logging in daily, completing tasks, and referring friends. The platform uses secure Google Firebase Authentication and we never share personal data with third parties.

How Does R U P Rewards Work?

Members earn points through our daily check-in system. After login, tap "Start Earning" to activate your daily bonus cycle. The base rate is 10 points per session. You can increase your earnings through four methods: maintaining a daily login streak for bonus multipliers, referring friends with your unique code for permanent speed boosts, completing sponsored tasks in our Task Center, and watching optional rewarded ads for extra points. Every new user who joins using your referral code grants both you and them a 20 point welcome bonus.

Is R U P Network Safe to Use?

Yes. R U P Network operates on a zero-investment model. We will never ask users for money, bank details, or payment to earn or withdraw rewards. The platform is currently in the community rewards phase. All balances are stored securely and linked to your Google account.
