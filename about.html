About RUPPY Network

Who We Are

RUPPY Network is a free-to-play rewards and entertainment application designed specifically for users in India. Founded in 2024, our core mission is to make earning digital rewards simple, safe, transparent, and entertaining for everyone. We believe that users should be rewarded for their time, attention, and engagement without requiring any form of financial investment, purchase, or risk. Our team is focused on building a sustainable platform where fun and fairness are the top priority.

What We Do

We provide a gamified ecosystem where users can collect free RUPPY coins, the virtual currency of our platform. The primary method to earn RUPPY is through our mining system, which allows a free claim every 3 hours. To keep the experience engaging, we offer multiple ways to boost rewards:

  • Daily Tasks: Complete simple activities like checking in or watching content to earn bonus coins.
  • Mining Boosts: Activate temporary boosts to increase your hourly mining rate.
  • Referral Program: Invite friends to join RUPPY Network and earn rewards when they are active.
  • Special Gifts: Claim surprise gift boxes that appear randomly for active users.
  • Leaderboards: Compete with other users to see who can collect the most RUPPY each week.

All activities are designed for entertainment purposes. Our platform runs 24/7 and allows users to track their progress in real-time through their personal dashboard.

How RUPPY Works

New users can sign up for free using just a name. There is no payment required at any stage. Once inside the app, your dashboard shows your current RUPPY balance, mining status, and available tasks. The mining circle on the main page indicates when your next free claim is ready. After claiming, a 3-hour cooldown timer begins. This system ensures fair distribution for all users and prevents spam or misuse of the platform.

Our Vision and Commitment

Our long-term vision is to become India's most trusted and user-friendly rewards and entertainment platform. We are committed to transparency, user safety, and data privacy. We will never sell user data or ask for sensitive information like bank details or passwords. Our goal is to build a large community where anyone can enjoy collecting points and competing with friends with zero financial investment.

Important Note & Disclaimer

RUPPY Network is an entertainment and gaming application. RUPPY points or coins are virtual rewards for in-app use only and have no real-world monetary value. They cannot be exchanged for cash, cryptocurrency, or any other currency outside the RUPPY Network app. This app is not a financial, investment, banking, or cryptocurrency platform. It is intended solely for entertainment. Please use the app responsibly.
