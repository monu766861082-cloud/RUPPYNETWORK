About RUPPY Network

Who We Are

RUPPY Network is a fun and engaging rewards application built for users in India. Our mission is to make earning rewards simple, safe, and entertaining for everyone.

What We Do

We provide users with free RUPPY rewards that can be collected every 3 hours. Users can boost their rewards through daily tasks, referral programs, and special bonus challenges. All activities are designed for entertainment purposes.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted rewards and entertainment platform where anyone can enjoy collecting points and competing with friends with zero investment.

Important Note

RUPPY Network is an entertainment and gaming application. RUPPY points are virtual rewards for in-app use only and have no real-world monetary value. This app is not a financial, investment, or cryptocurrency platform.
