Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to RUPPY Network. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our rewards app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo you provide during registration
  • Usage Data: App activity, rewards collected, tasks completed, and referral activity
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for app functionality
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your game progress and preferences on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Operating and maintaining your account
  • Tracking your rewards balance and game progress
  • Displaying leaderboards and referral statistics
  • Sending notifications about rewards if you enable them
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring fair play

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these cases:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Advertising Partners: We display ads from Google AdSense and Monetag. These partners may collect anonymous usage data per their policies

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is primarily stored locally on your device using browser storage. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage and may use cookies for:

  • Keeping you logged in
  • Saving your preferences
  • Analytics to understand app usage
  • Advertising by our ad partners

6. Third-Party Links

Our app contains links to social media platforms (Telegram, Facebook, X) for task completion. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

RUPPY Network is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information in Settings
  • Delete your account and data by logging out and clearing browser data
  • Disable notifications in Settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please visit our Contact Page.

RUPPY Network is a rewards and entertainment application. RUPPY points have no monetary value and are for in-app use only.
