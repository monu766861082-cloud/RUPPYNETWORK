Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026 | Effective Date: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

Welcome to RUPPY Network. This Privacy Policy describes how RUPPY Network collects, uses, processes, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application and related website services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. By using RUPPY Network, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect - Personal Data

When you register or interact with our Service, we may collect personal information that can be used to identify you. This includes your name, email address, and profile photo if you choose to provide them. This information is used to create and manage your account and to personalize your experience.

3. Information We Collect - Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how the Service is accessed and used. Usage Data includes your rewards balance, tasks completed, mining activity timestamps, referral statistics, leaderboards participation, pages visited, time spent on features, and interaction with advertisements. This helps us understand user behavior and improve functionality.

4. Information We Collect - Device Data

We collect information about the device you use to access RUPPY Network. This may include your device type, model, unique device identifiers, operating system and version, mobile network information, browser type, and IP address. Device Data is used for analytics, security, fraud prevention, and to ensure app compatibility.

5. Information We Collect - Location Data

We do not collect precise GPS location data. We may derive approximate location such as country or city from your IP address to comply with regional laws, show relevant offers, and prevent fraudulent activity. You can use the app without enabling location services.

6. Information We Collect - Local Storage

RUPPY Network uses browser local storage and session storage to save your game progress, User ID, rewards balance, and app preferences directly on your device. This allows the app to function offline and retain your data between sessions. Clearing your browser data will reset this information.

7. How We Use Your Information - Core Service

The primary use of your information is to operate and maintain your account. We use your data to track your RUPPY points, enable mining sessions, validate task completions, process referrals, and display your rank on leaderboards. Without this data, core features of the app cannot function.

8. How We Use Your Information - Improvement

We analyze Usage Data and Device Data to understand how users interact with RUPPY Network. This analysis helps us fix bugs, improve app performance, develop new features, and optimize user experience. All analysis is performed on aggregated or de-identified data where possible.

9. How We Use Your Information - Communication

We may use your email to send important service updates, security alerts, and support responses. If you enable notifications, we may send push notifications about mining availability or new tasks. You can disable notifications anytime in Settings. We do not send marketing emails without consent.

10. How We Use Your Information - Security

Your data helps us detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents. We monitor for multiple accounts, bot activity, emulator usage, and other violations of our Terms and Conditions. This protects the integrity of the rewards system for all users.

11. Legal Basis for Processing Data

We process your data based on: your consent for optional features like notifications, contractual necessity to provide the Service you requested, legitimate interests for security and app improvement, and compliance with legal obligations. You may withdraw consent for optional processing at any time.

12. Data Sharing - Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf. This includes cloud hosting providers, analytics services, and customer support tools. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes. They cannot sell your data.

15. Data Sharing - Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities. This includes meeting national security or law enforcement requirements, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users or the public.

18. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies for keeping you logged in, saving preferences, analytics, and advertising. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features may not function properly without cookies.

19. Local Storage & Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use HTML5 local storage and session storage. These technologies are similar to cookies but can store larger amounts of data and persist longer. We use them to store your User ID, points balance, and app state. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary for sync.

20. Data Storage and Security Measures

Your game data is primarily stored locally on your device. Account data is stored on secure cloud servers with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no internet transmission or storage system is 100 percent secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

21. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Local storage data persists until you clear it. Account data is retained while your account is active. If your account is inactive for 12 months, we may delete it and associated data. You can request deletion at any time.

22. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, or country where data protection laws may differ. Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of information represents your agreement to that transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

23. Children's Privacy COPPA Compliance

RUPPY Network is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected data from children under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

24. Your Rights - Access & Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. You can view and update your profile information directly in the app Settings. If you believe any information we hold is inaccurate, you can request correction by contacting us.

25. Your Rights - Deletion & Erasure

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can delete most data by logging out and clearing your browser data. You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. Since most data is stored locally on your device, you already have access. For server-side data, contact us and we will provide an export where technically feasible.
